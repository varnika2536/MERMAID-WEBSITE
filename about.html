Our Story

We are Varnika and Aradhya, two best friends and fashion lovers who started MERMAID with a dream to create magical and beautiful clothes for every girl who loves the ocean, colors, and creativity.

Our Mission

At MERMAID, we believe that fashion is for everyone! We design clothes that are fun, colorful, and comfortable. Whether it's a sunny beach day or a fun school event, MERMAID outfits make you feel confident and happy.
